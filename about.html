About UMHC

The Upper Midwest Honors Council (UMHC) is a consortium of honors programs across the Midwest dedicated to fostering academic excellence, leadership, and community engagement among students.

Our Mission

To support and advance honors education by facilitating collaboration, professional development, and scholarly exchange among member institutions.

Our History

Founded in 1990, UMHC began as a small gathering of five universities. Today, we represent over a dozen institutions committed to enriching the undergraduate experience.
