Honors Opportunities

Discover scholarships, internships, study abroad programs, and leadership experiences curated for honors students.

Scholarships

  • UMHC Excellence Scholarship: Up to $2,000 for outstanding thesis projects.
  • Midwest Leadership Award: $1,500 for leadership initiatives in regional chapters.

Internships

  • Daktronics Student Intern Program – Electronics & software design.
  • U.S. Department of State Internship – Policy & diplomacy.

Study Abroad

  • Capstone Exchange: Semester abroad at partner institutions in Germany and Japan.
  • Global Honors Program: 6-week summer research program in Europe.

Leadership Experiences

  • Regional Council Delegate: Represent your chapter at the annual UMHC summit.
  • Peer Mentor Program: Mentor incoming honors students at DSU.
